What is PromptPad?

A privacy-first workspace for preparing, refining, and sharing AI prompts.

When you're copy-pasting prompts into ChatGPT, Claude, or another LLM, it's easy to overshare. PromptPad is your sanity check—and gives you a hand to keep your prompts clean, lean, and safe to share, without losing their soul. Read more.
